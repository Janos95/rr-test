Visualizing a Spatial Graph

As before, spatial graphs can be visualized with only two lines of code:

std::vector<glm::vec3> nodes;
std::vector<std::pair<int, int>> edges;
rr::SpatialGraph* sg = rr::make_spatial_graph("spatial graph", nodes, edges);
